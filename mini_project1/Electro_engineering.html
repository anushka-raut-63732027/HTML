B.Tech. (Electronics and Communication Engineering)

Programme Overview

B.Tech Electronics and Communication Engineering is a four-year undergraduate programme that aims to impart fundamental knowledge and understanding of circuit signals and the technical process for developing electronics and communication devices.

Programme Outcomes

  • PO 1:Apply basic knowledge of mathematics, science & engineering.

  • PO 2:Identify, formulate, analyze and solve engineering problems.

  • PO 3:Design and develop systems/ processes to meet the desired specifications.

  • PO 4: Use of research-based knowledge to design and conduct experiments, analysis and interpret data to provide valid conclusions.

  • PO 5:Apply the techniques, resources and modern engineering tools required for Electronics Engineering applications.
