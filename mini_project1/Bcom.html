B.Com (Bachelor of Commerce)

Programme Overview

Bachelor of Commerce popularly known as B. Com is of three years duration. It is during this period that the students learn about financial accounting and they develop skills pertaining to different kinds of business transactions. Students are also taught the basics related to micro and macro-economics.

Programme Outcomes

  • Get to know theoretical framework of accounting - Students will be taught about how to use the financial accounting information, functions as well as advantages of accounting.

  • Knowledge about important Business legislations - Candidates get to know all the significant business legislations and its importance.
  • Know the concept of Macro Economics - Students are taught all the concepts of microeconomics with special focus on consumer behaviour. They also get an understanding about the supply side of the market through the production and cost behaviour of firms.
