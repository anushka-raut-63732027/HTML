Welcome to Pillai College

ABOUT

Pillai College of enginerring was established in 1998 by Dr. K. M. Vasudevan Pillai, Chairman and CEO, MES whose vision was to provide students of Navi Mumbai with quality education in a high-tech atmosphere. It is situated in a 10 acre land surrounded by natural beauty and supported by the best infrastructure facilities, a state-of-the-art conference room, well-equipped computer labs and well-stocked libraries. Our vision is “Education for All”. To fulfill our vision, we welcome students from diverse backgrounds irrespective of region, religion, caste, economic strata and academic performance.

MISSION

  • Achieving excellence in education by maintaining high standards of teaching, shaping talents of students, moulding their character and imbibing in them a sense of civic responsibility.

  • Empowering students to become able, responsible citizens by imparting quality education and inculcating sound values.
