B.Sc. (Bachelor of Science - Nursing)

Programme Overview

B.Sc. Nursing is a 4-year undergraduate course comprising 8 semesters which prepares aspirants to become a professional registered nurse to serve humanity through medical treatment. The course covers topics such as anatomy, physiology, biochemistry, nutrition, pharmacology, pathology and genetics, medical-surgical nursing, phycology, microbiology, operation theater technique, child health nursing and many more.

Programme Outcomes

  • Effective caring service - Students will be able to provide compassionate and effective nursing caring service with a holistic approach to the patient based on the client's health condition. Bsc Nursing graduates are competent to provide medical-surgical, pediatric, psychiatric and maternal nursing care. Accountable to provide community health nursing services through inter and multidisciplinary health teams.

  • Develop Skills - Build communication skill, time management organization and good at teamwork and collaboration
  • Travel opportunity - BSc Nursing graduates have the opportunity to travel and serve humanity through medical treatment and uplift the status of nursing as a profession.
