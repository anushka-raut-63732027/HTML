BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration)

Programme Overview

The Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) is a three-year undergraduate programme that focuses on imparting entrepreneurial and managerial skills to the students. During the course, the students will be trained in management and leadership and be acquainted with the various aspects of business administration, marketing trends, market, accounting, economics and more through theoretical and practical projects.

Programme Outcomes

  • Remember management concepts, theories, models and key business terms.

  • Understand management principles and practices in the organizational context, to achieve organizational goals.

  • Apply optimum solutions to problems in the field of Business Management.

  • Use sustainable and ethical business practices in the Contemporary business scenario.

  • Analyze the need for and engage in lifelong learning in the field of business management.
