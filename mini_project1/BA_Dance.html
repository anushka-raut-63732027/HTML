BA Dance (Bachelor of Arts)

Programme Overview

Bachelor of Arts in Dance is a full time three years undergraduate programme where the students are taught about dance and drama, acting etc. Students get to learn the theoretical as well as practical aspects pertaining to dance. In these three years, they are also taught the dancing technique and history..

Programme Outcomes

  • Acquire Textual and Practical Knowledge - In these three years, students will get to learn about the practical and textual knowledge related to the dance

  • Understand the significance of Music - While pursuing the course, students will get to know all the details about the music and its significance in relation to dance.

  • Become Expressive - Creativity has to be at its best while performing a dance. Hence, the students need to be expressive as it through their facial and other expressions, they will be able to communicate the meaning of the dance that they are performing.
